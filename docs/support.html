Common questions

How do I add my masjid to Masjidly?

Email us the masjid name, city, and website (or Instagram) at support@masjidly.app. We'll add it to the scraping pipeline, usually within a few days.

An event is wrong / missing / outdated. Can you fix it?

Yes — please email support@masjidly.app with the event name, date, and the correct details (or a link to the masjid's official post) and we'll update it.

How do I turn off notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications inside Masjidly and toggle the categories you want. You can also turn them off entirely from iOS Settings → Notifications → Masjidly.

How do I delete my account?

Inside the app: Settings → Account → Delete my account. Full details at Delete your account.

How do I change my location / home masjid?

Open Settings → Location. You can grant/revoke location permission, or change your home city manually.

How do I report a bug?

Email support@masjidly.app and include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • What you were doing when it happened
  • A screenshot if you have one

How are events chosen?

Masjidly aggregates events from each masjid's public website, newsletter, and official social-media posts. Anything you see in the app is something the masjid has already announced publicly. If you're a masjid admin and want control over your listings, email us and we'll set you up.

About Masjidly

Masjidly started because finding a weekend halaqa shouldn't require scrolling through 11 different Instagram accounts. We're a tiny, Muslim-owned team trying to make it easy for our community to show up for each other. If that sounds useful to you, email us — we'd love to hear what you want to see next.

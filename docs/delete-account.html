Delete your account

You can delete your Masjidly account at any time. Here's how.

Option 1 — From inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Masjidly.
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom-right).
  3. Scroll down to Account.
  4. Tap Delete my account.
  5. Confirm twice. Your account and all associated data are queued for permanent deletion.

Option 2 — By email

If you can't open the app, email support@masjidly.app from the email address tied to your account with the subject "Delete my account". We'll confirm and delete it within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your email and password hash
  • All followed masjids and scholars
  • All saved events and RSVPs
  • All notification preferences and your push token
  • Any referral codes you created

What does not get deleted

  • Public event listings (they're aggregated from masjids' own public posts and aren't tied to your identity)
  • Aggregate, non-identifiable analytics

Deletion is permanent. We cannot restore an account after it's been deleted.
